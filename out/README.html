Overview

WhatsApp Status lets you share temporary updates that disappear after 24 hours. You can share photos, videos, text, voice notes, and music. Your status updates are end-to-end encrypted, and visible only to the contacts you choose.

This guide explains how to update your WhatsApp status on Android devices using different types.

Types of Status

WhatsApp offers several types of status updates:

  • Text: Post messages or quotes.
  • Photo: Share images from your camera or gallery.
  • Video: Share video clips and recordings.
  • Voice: Record and share audio.
  • Music: Add songs to your media.

Updating Status on Android Devices

This section explains how to update your status with photos, videos, text, voice notes, and music on Android devices.

Prerequisites

Before you start, check the following requirements:

  • Enable permissions: Allow WhatsApp access to your camera, microphone, and gallery.
  • Internet connection: Use Wi-Fi or mobile data.
  • Update the app: Install the latest version of WhatsApp.

Status Privacy Options

In WhatsApp, the Status privacy feature lets you control who can see your status using the following options:

  • My contacts: Share your updates with all your contacts.
  • My contacts except...: Share with everyone except the people you select.
  • Only share with...: Share only with the specific contacts you choose.

Using Text

To update your status with text, perform the following steps:

  1. On your device, open WhatsApp.
  2. At the bottom of the screen, tap Updates.
  3. On the Updates screen, tap the Pencil icon.
  4. On the Text screen, type your message. To format or edit your text, tap and hold the text to select, then choose the following options:
  • Standard tools: Tap Translate, Cut, Copy, or Undo.
  • Format: Tap the three-dot button to select Bold, Italic, Strikethrough, Monospace, or Share.
  1. At the bottom left, tap Status to choose your contacts.
  2. At the bottom right, tap the Send icon to share your update.

You update a text status.

Using Photo

To update your status with a photo, perform the following steps:

  1. On your device, open WhatsApp.
  2. At the bottom of the screen, tap Updates.
  3. On the Updates screen, tap the Camera icon or Add status.
  4. In your gallery, select a photo.
  5. At the top of the photo screen, use the following tools to customize your photo:

  • Tap to adjust color, alignment, and background.

  • Tap to add location, time, emojis, and stickers.

  • Tap to draw.

  • Tap to add a song.

  • Tap to rotate or resize your photo.

  1. Below the photo on the left, tap Add a caption to type a message.
  2. Below the photo on the right, tap the @ icon to mention your contacts.
  3. At the bottom left, tap Status to choose you contacts.
  4. At the bottom right, tap the Send icon to share your update.

You update a photo status.

Using Voice

To update your status with voice, perform the following steps:

  1. On your device, open WhatsApp.
  2. At the bottom of the screen, tap Updates.
  3. On the Updates screen, tap the Pencil icon.
  4. In the Text screen, tap Voice.
  5. In the Voice screen, record your message:

  • Record: Tap and hold the Microphone icon.

  • Stop: Release the Microphone icon.

  1. In the voice message area, manage your recording:

  • Review: Tap the Play icon to listen your message.

  • Remove: Tap the Delete icon to discard your message.

  1. At the top of the screen, tap to change the background colour.
  2. At the bottom left, tap Status to choose you contacts.
  3. At the bottom right, tap the Send icon to share your update.

You update a voice status.
